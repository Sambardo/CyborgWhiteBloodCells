Instructions:
Aiming up or down while standing still fires in that direction.
Aiming up or down while moving fires diagonally in that direction.
Kill the enemy diseases in the body.
Collect Gems for a higher score.
Collect power ups to help you survive.

KeyBoard controls:
Jump: X, K, Space
Shoot: Z, J, Alt
Move Left: A, Left Arrow
Move Right: D, Right Arrow
Down: S, Down Arrow
Up: W, Up Arrow
continue: Space
Pause: Esc

Gamepad controls:
Jump: A
Shoot: X
Up, Down, Left, Right: Dpad or Thumbstick (untested)
Continue: A
